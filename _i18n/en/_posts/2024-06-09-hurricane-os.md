## The OS Built for the Edge

HurricaneOS is a purpose-built operating system designed from the ground up for decentralized, low-bandwidth edge environments.

## Design Philosophy

Minimal attack surface with a stripped-down kernel, deterministic scheduling, and hardware-level security primitives.

## Key Capabilities

- Sub-second boot time with persistent state
- Over-the-air updates optimized for sparse connectivity
- Hardware-accelerated cryptography and enclave support
